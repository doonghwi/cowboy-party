## Full Description (Play 4000 chars / App Store body)

🤠 **Reload? Defend? Or shoot?**

Cowboy is a Western standoff duel for 2–6 players sitting in a circle, each picking one action
**simultaneously** every turn. Read your rivals, stockpile bullets, and pull the trigger at the
perfect moment. The last one standing wins!

**■ Simple rules, deep mind games**
• Reload: stockpile bullets (up to 6)
• Defend: block attacks aimed at you this turn
• Shoot: spend 1 bullet to snipe a player
• Super-shot: when fully loaded, a finishing blow that pierces defense
Everyone reveals at the same time — it all comes down to reading who shoots and who blocks.

**■ 17 distinct characters**
Sniper (pierces defense), Doctor (survive one fatal hit), Hunter (reflecting trap),
Pacifist (win by reloading alone), Russian Roulette, Dual Gun, Voodoo (curse), Shadow (hides
info)… each one plays completely differently.

**■ Online & offline**
• Quick Match: one tap to meet anyone and duel instantly
• Create a room / invite friends by code or link (up to 6)
• Solo play vs. computer (bots)
• Unlock characters, earn coins, daily rewards, monthly rankings

**■ Light, anywhere**
A round takes just minutes. Enjoy a one-bullet mind game with friends — or cowboys worldwide.
