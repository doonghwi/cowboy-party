## Full Description (Play 4000 chars / App Store body)

🤠 **Shoot? Block? — Everyone reveals at once!**

Will your friend defend, or aim at you? Cowboy is a Western standoff duel for 2–6 players
around a circular table, each picking one action **simultaneously** every turn.
A round takes 3–5 minutes; the rules take 30 seconds. Read your rivals, stockpile bullets,
and pull the trigger at the perfect moment. The last one standing wins!

**■ Simple rules, deep mind games**
• Reload: stockpile bullets (up to 6)
• Defend: block every attack aimed at you this turn
• Shoot: spend 1 bullet to snipe a player
• Super-shot: when fully loaded, a finishing blow that pierces defense
Everyone reveals at once — get shot and the screen shakes with a thump you can feel.

**■ 15 distinct characters**
Sniper (pierces defense), Doctor (survive one fatal hit), Hunter (reflecting trap),
Pacifist (win by reloading alone), Russian Roulette (fate's 50:50), Dual Gun, Voodoo (curse),
Shadow (hides info), Paparazzi (peek)… each plays completely differently.
Unlock them one by one with coins from wins and daily rewards!

**■ Online & offline**
• Quick Match: one tap to meet anyone and duel instantly
• Create a room / invite friends by code or link (up to 6, password rooms supported)
• Solo play vs. computer bots, each with its own personality
• Everyone down at once? A reaction-speed showdown picks the true winner

**■ Reasons to come back every day**
• Daily missions + 7-day check-in rewards keep the coins flowing
• Weekly rankings — reset every Monday, so anyone can chase #1 this week
• Last week's champions get a hall-of-fame spot

A round takes just minutes. Enjoy a one-bullet mind game with friends — or cowboys worldwide.
